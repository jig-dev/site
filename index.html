A tool to define, compute, and monitor your metrics

Your data, wherever it is.

Jig runs locally, so it can download data from your internal REST APIs and databases without exposing anything to the internet or requiring costly import procedures.

Any metric. Really.

There is always something special about your business. Whether you want to exclude a region from a metric, or smooth out your numbers over the holiday season, Jig packs in a full data science platform so it does not constrain you in any way.

Interactive dashboards.

Jig comes with charts and tables to visualize your data, but also with buttons, filters and inputs of all kinds to bring interactivity to your dashboard.
