Your company, your metrics

There is always something special about your business. Whether you need to exclude some region from a metric, or smooth out your numbers over the holiday season, whether you want to assign a bigger weight to a particular client, or answer an investor's specific question, Jig doesn't constrain you in any way. As a full data science engine, you can compute anything you need.

Full data science platform in a popular language

Jig leverages the Observable runtime to compute yo
ur metrics and create your dashboards. Like spreadsheets, Jig's dashboards are made of computable cells that transform and display data. But Jig's cells can also contain buttons, filters and inputs to create powerful interactive dashboards. Chances are that you already have a Javascript developer in your team who will feel right at home in Jig.

Productivity first

Jig dashboards are running in a "reactive live" environment, which means that anything on the dashboard can be changed at any time. Just open the cell's code, type in the change and voila!

Data from all your sources, on your own computer

You can work on data from anywhere including local files, databases, and REST web apis. Your data stays on your computer and is never sent to us. It's both more reliable and more secure that way.
